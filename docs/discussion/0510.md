Orbit Wars top-10% daily episode replay datasets
I've published a per-day series of replay datasets covering Orbit Wars, one dataset per UTC day from 2026-04-16 through 2026-05-04. Each dataset contains the highest-quality matchups of that day - useful for opponent analysis, imitation learning, or just spectating top-of-leaderboard play.

Methodology
For each UTC day:

Query for every Orbit Wars episode created that day.
For each episode, sum both agents' post-episode rating (UpdatedScore). This biases toward matchups where strong bots played strong bots.
Keep the top 10% of episodes by that summed rating.
Download replay JSONs in priority order (highest sum first).
Stop as soon as the next replay would push the total over 20 GiB (most days are well under).
Bundle the chosen replays and publish.
Each dataset contains:

episodes.tar.gz — the replay JSONs, extracts to episodes/{episode_id}.json
manifest.csv — episode_id, create_time, sum_score, min_score, avg_score, scores, submission_ids, size_bytes (in priority order)
